Recipe #1: Sinigang

Sinigang is a sour soup native to the Philippines. This recipe uses pork as the main ingredient, but other proteins like beef, shrimp, and fish can also be used.

Ingredients

  • Main Ingredients & Alternatives:
    • Pork Belly
    • Chicken
    • Beef
    • Shrimp
    • Fish
  • Young Tamarind
  • Water Spinach
  • String Beans
  • Eggplant
  • Daikon Radish
  • Long Green Pepper

How to Cook Sinigang

  1. Extract flavors from the tamarind.
  2. Boil the tamarind and cook the meat.
  3. Add the vegetables sequentially.
  4. Season it and then add the remaining ingredients.
  5. Serve it hot.
  6. Enjoy!
